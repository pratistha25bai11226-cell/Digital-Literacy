# Task 5 – Cybercrime Awareness


This task builds awareness about cybercrime types common among college students in India, and equips peers with actionable prevention strategies.

---

## Part A – Case Study

### Crime Type: UPI / Online Payment Fraud

The case study covers the widely reported **OLX Army Officer Scam** — a realistic scenario in which a college student selling a laptop is tricked into authorising a UPI payment under the false impression he is *receiving* money.

| Section | Summary |
|---|---|
| **What the crime is** | UPI collect request fraud — victim authorises an outgoing payment thinking it is incoming |
| **How it happens** | Trust-building → urgency → fake collect request → victim enters PIN → money debited |
| **Who is targeted** | Students on OLX/Quikr, first-time UPI users, job seekers, senior citizens |
| **Consequences** | Immediate financial loss, psychological distress, difficult/impossible reversal |
